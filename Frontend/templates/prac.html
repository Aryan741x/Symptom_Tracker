Prognosis: Allergy

An allergy is an immune system response to a foreign substance that's not typically harmful to your body.They can include certain foods, pollen, or pet dander. Your immune system's job is to keep you healthy by fighting harmful pathogens.Allergies, also known as allergic diseases, are a number of conditions caused by hypersensitivity of the immune system to typically harmless substances in the environment. These diseases include hay fever, food allergies, atopic dermatitis, allergic asthma, and anaphylaxis.

PRECAUTION

1. Avoid known allergens
2. Keep a clean environment
3. Monitor symptoms
4. Follow a doctor's advice

MEDICATION

1. Antihistamines
2. Decongestants
3. Corticosteroids
4. Immunotherapy
